Our Programs

Education pathways designed for adult learners

High School Equivalency

HiSET / GED® Preparation

In‑person and online classes available.
Instruction in Reading, Writing, Math, Science and Social Studies.

Literacy & Foundations

Adult Learning Foundations

Improve your reading, writing, and basic math skills with learners at a similar level. Do you need help with literacy and the fundamentals of education to earn your high school equivalency (formerly known as GED)? We're here to help you get there! We currently offer a hybrid class to accommodate your needs: Online classes meet Monday to Wednesday: 10 a.m. to noon OR 5 p.m. to 7 p.m. In-person classes meet on Thursday: 10 a.m. to noon OR 5 p.m. to 7 p.m.

Career and College Transition Prep

All students have access to a self-paced Career Pathways Toolkit in Google Classroom.

Students explore career options, participate in field trips to local businesses and post-secondary programs, and receive personalized support throughout the year for career and job transitions.
