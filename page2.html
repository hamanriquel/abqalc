Student Success Stories

Shante Bryant- Class of 2023

"I remember walking into class on my first day. Nadia was my teacher at that time. She was so welcoming. I really liked the way she taught. Anyway after a few months we changed teachers. That is when Mr.Enka took over. He was pretty cool he pushed me to really focus. I almost gave up at the end not wanting to take my math test, being that I failed the first time. I remember him telling me to go ahead and take it that hes confident that I would pass. So about a month and half later I went to retest and I did pass. So I guess when you study and really focus and believe in yourself anything is possible. Now I am enrolled at CNM going for my Medical Assistance certificate. So thank you all here at ABQ-ALS. Thanks to Matt because he was always there helping along the way as well!!"
