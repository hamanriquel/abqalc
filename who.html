Our Core Values

Student-Focused

We are dedicated to providing a safe and supportive learning environment where students can achieve their goals.

Community-Centered

We work closely with community partners to ensure access to valuable resources for our students.

Teamwork & Collaboration

Together we support one another, share knowledge, and create meaningful opportunities.

Compassion & Empathy

We meet every learner where they are and respect each individual journey.

Adaptable & Flexible

We adjust our programs and instruction to meet changing needs and circumstances.

Equity & Inclusiveness

We value diverse perspectives and ensure access to education for all.

Our Model

Community-Based Learning

Albuquerque Adult Learning Center, Inc. (ABQ-ALC) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization established in 2010. Formerly known as Albuquerque GED®, Inc., ABQ-ALC brings over a decade of experience supporting adult learners through high school equivalency preparation, post-secondary transition, and career readiness services.

As a community-based, grass-roots organization, we focus on serving adults in some of the most under-served communities throughout the Albuquerque Metro area.
